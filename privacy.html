4. 資料儲存與安全 Data Storage & Security

  • 您的資料儲存於 Supabase 雲端平台（PostgreSQL 資料庫），伺服器位於受保護的資料中心
  • 所有資料傳輸皆使用 HTTPS 加密
  • 密碼使用 bcrypt 雜湊演算法加密儲存
  • 資料庫實施行級安全策略（Row-Level Security），確保使用者僅能存取自己的資料
  • 圖片與影片儲存於 Supabase Storage，採用安全的存取控制
  • Your data is stored on the Supabase cloud platform (PostgreSQL database) on protected servers
  • All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS
  • Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing algorithm
  • Database enforces Row-Level Security (RLS) policies to ensure users can only access their own data
  • Images and videos are stored in Supabase Storage with secure access controls

  • 經您明確同意

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party. We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes. Your data may only be disclosed in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or lawful government requests

當您選擇透過 Instagram 登入或匯入內容時：

  • 我們僅取得您主動授權的資料（使用者名稱、頭像、簡介、媒體內容）

When you choose to log in via Instagram or import content:

  • We only access data you explicitly authorize (username, avatar, bio, media content)
  • Instagram data is used solely to create your Outfo profile and import outfit content you select

  • Instagram access tokens are stored locally on your device only and are not uploaded to our servers
  • You can delete all Instagram-related data at any time via "Settings → Delete Account"
  • You can also revoke Outfo's access in your Instagram settings

  • Your account data is retained while you use the Service

  • You can permanently delete your account at any time via "Settings → Delete Account"
  • Account deletion will permanently remove: profile data, all posts and videos, comments, likes, follow relationships, collections, messages, notifications, and all related data
  • Account deletion is irreversible

11. 使用者內容管理 User-Generated Content Moderation

為維護社群環境，我們提供以下機制：

  • 檢舉：使用者可長按任何貼文、影片或留言檢舉違規內容
  • 封鎖：使用者可在個人檔案頁封鎖其他使用者，被封鎖者的內容將從動態、搜尋、個人頁完全隱藏
  • 處理時效：我們承諾在 24 小時內處理所有 UGC 檢舉
  • 對明顯違規內容（仇恨言論、騷擾、色情、違法、智財侵權）將立即移除
  • 對騷擾、仇恨言論、明顯違法內容採零容忍政策

To maintain a safe community, we provide the following mechanisms:

  • Reporting: Users can long-press any post, Reel, or comment to report violating content
  • Blocking: Users can block other users on profile pages; blocked users' content is fully hidden from feed, search, and profile views
  • Response time: We commit to handling all UGC reports within 24 hours
  • Obviously violating content (hate speech, harassment, sexual content, illegal content, IP infringement) will be removed immediately
  • Zero tolerance for harassment, hate speech, and obviously illegal content
